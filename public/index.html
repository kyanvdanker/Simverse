What is PHYSIX

A Physics Lab In Your Browser

PHYSIX is a complete drag-and-drop physics simulator built for students, teachers, and curious minds. No downloads, no sign-ups, no code.

🔵

Drag-and-drop objects

Spheres, boxes, rockets, planets, black holes — drag them onto the canvas and they obey real physics immediately.

⚡

Apply real forces

Gravity, Coulomb, Lorentz, spring, drag, thermal, nuclear — layer as many forces as you need on any body.

📊

Live graphs & readouts

Position, velocity, energy, angular momentum — visualised in real time on live graph panels with formula overlays.

🌌

Scale from atoms to galaxies

Simulate a Coulomb orbit at the nanometre scale or a full solar system spanning billions of kilometres.

📚

Curriculum-linked presets

25+ presets map directly to BINAS, IB, GCSE, and AP physics curricula — load a preset and start teaching immediately.

🚀

Speed control

Slow time to 0.001× to study collisions frame-by-frame, or fast-forward 100× to watch orbital decay over centuries.
