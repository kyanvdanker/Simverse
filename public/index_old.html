Getting started

THREE STEPS

No sign-up. No download. Open a browser and simulate anything.

01
Open PhysiX
Click "Launch PhysiX" above. It loads instantly in your browser — desktop or tablet. No account, no install.
02
Build Your Sim
Drag objects from the left panel. Drop force tiles onto bodies. Adjust mass, velocity, radius, charge, and more from the right panel.
03
Press Play
Hit PLAY and watch physics unfold. Adjust simulation speed from 0.01× to 100,000×. Watch real-time graphs update as bodies interact.
